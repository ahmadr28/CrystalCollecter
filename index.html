Crystal Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a random amount of points to your score.
You win the game by matching your total score to the random number below, you lose if your total goes above the number generated.
The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it
The Game restarts every time you either win or lose.
